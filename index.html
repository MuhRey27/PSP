ABOUT US

PT. Pilar Sinergi Perkasa

Established in 202, PT. Pilar Sinergi Perkasa (PSP) is an integrated engineering, procurement, and construction company. PSP provides competencies covering end to end solutions comprised of Concept Development, EPC Service, Operatjon & Maintenace to client in wide range of markets including upstream, midstream and downstream Fertilizer, Power Plant, Mining, Mineral Processing, Copper & Gold Smelter, Oil & Gas, Petrochemical, Pulp & Papper, Environment and General Industrial.

PSP maintains high standards for health, safety & environment and committed to delivering quality projects with advanced technology on budget and on schedule to the complete satisfaction to our clients.
